About Us

Texican Apparel is a small business that was started in 2021. We are a family owned business that is dedicated to providing quality products to our customers. We specialize in hats and stickers that cater to those that love BBQ and Texan culture. Our products are designed to be unique and to stand out and embody the Texan spirit. We are always looking for new ideas and designs to add to our collection. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and to making sure that our customers are satisfied with their purchases. Thank you for supporting our business!
